Regional Report, April 2020

Published May 1, 2020

In April, the average temperature in the Kalmont Mountain Range was 6C with a high of 15 and a low for the month of -10. Out of 30 days in April, a record 20 of them were rainy.

Despite the weather, the Kalmont Ski Resort welcomed 120 000 guests this month, including the esteemed _____..

We are happy that ___ enjoyed their stay, and hope to welcome them back to Kalmont again soon.

This month, the KMSRT celebrated its 50th anniversary of service, keeping Kalmont’s visitors safe since 1970. A big thank you to the safety and rescue staff who work their hardest to ensure the safety of everyone. In further celebration of this milestone, all ski and hiking passes will be discounted 10% off until the end of May. The Kalmont Resort Lodge Grill is also offering a great deal this month: Two can dine for $19.99 on all steaks and rib platters. You won’t want to miss it!
