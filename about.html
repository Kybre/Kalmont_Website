Mission Statement

KMSRT is a global community, welcoming people from all over the world, every discipline, every culture, who seek to make a difference to better our community. We passionately believe in protecting anyone and everyone, and wish to put our lives on the line for the sake of safety and security. Through this, we want to create a safe and welcoming environment for visitors and staff at the ski resort, and wish for everyone to have the best experience they can at Kalmont Ski Resort.

Founded in 2008 by Frieza Freeze, Kalmont's Safety and Rescue Team has gone from a simple team of volunteer workers to a full-scale operation spanning the entire mountain range. And we are still continuing to expand. We are constantly looking to add more members to our team; to bring new things to our operation, to promote proper survival tips to all members of Kalmont, and above all, make Kalmont the safest environment it can be.
